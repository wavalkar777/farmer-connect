🌾 Farming Tips

Explore modern farming methods, organic practices, and seasonal strategies designed to increase productivity and promote sustainable agriculture.

🚜 Modern Techniques

  • Crop rotation and mixed cropping to improve soil fertility
  • Drip and sprinkler irrigation for water conservation
  • Use of sensors, IoT, and weather data in precision farming

🍀 Organic Methods

  • Composting kitchen and farm waste into nutrient-rich manure
  • Bio-pesticides like neem oil and garlic spray
  • Using green manure and cover crops to enrich the soil

📅 Seasonal Advice

  • Monsoon crops: Rice, maize, pulses, soybeans
  • Winter crops: Wheat, mustard, peas, carrots
  • Summer crops: Millets, cucumbers, gourds

🐛 Pest Control & Irrigation Guides

  • Manual removal and natural traps for pests
  • Drip irrigation setup and maintenance
  • Using cow dung slurry and compost tea for plant protection
